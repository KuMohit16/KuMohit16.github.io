I'm Mohit Kumar Shersiya — a full-stack content creator based in Jaipur with 5 years of hands-on experience across every stage of the content pipeline. I don't just edit videos; I build them from the ground up. From the first spark of an idea to the final exported frame, I handle it all — ideation, scripting, visualizing, shooting, and editing.

I've been in front of the camera as many times as I've been behind it, which gives me a unique edge: I understand what it takes to perform, not just to frame and cut. Whether it's a cinematic short film, a long-form YouTube documentary, a punchy social media reel, or an emotional wedding film — I bring both creative vision and technical precision to every project. My editing weapon of choice is DaVinci Resolve.
